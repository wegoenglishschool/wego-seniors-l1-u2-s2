SLANGS Slang or Not Slang?
8 min
"I'm ___ and this is ___."
Use this to introduce someone to others in a friendly way.
Bestie
Your very best friend — the person you are closest to.
My better half
Your romantic partner — your husband, wife or significant other.
My plus one
The person you bring to an event — your date or companion.
My partner in crime
Your fun accomplice — the friend you do everything with.
